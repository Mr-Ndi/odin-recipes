lasagna


description


a flat and expanded pasta sheet, traditionally made in Italy with Parmigiano-Reggiano (Parmesan cheese), Béchamel sauce (white sauce), and ragù (a meat-based sauce).

ingredients


    The most common ingredient in lasagna dishes are:
  • lasagna noodles
  • Parmesan cheese
  • garlic
  • olive oil
  • ricotta cheese
  • mozzarella cheese
  • egg
  • onion
  • tomatoes
  • butter
  • flour

Steps

  1. Heat the oven to 400ºF.
  2. Brown the beef and onion
  3. Prepare the baking dish and assemble the meat sauce
  4. Begin layering the lasagna
  5. Continue layering the lasagna.
  6. Bake the lasagna for 1 hour.
  7. Sprinkle with the remaining mozzarella and finish baking
  8. Cool the lasagna for 15 minutes
